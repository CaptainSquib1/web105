Dolphin Pod

Whales are renowned for their intelligence and complex social structures. They are known to communicate through a range of vocalizations and have even been observed working together in coordinated hunting strategies. Baleen whales, in particular, are crucial for maintaining the health of marine ecosystems. They help control the population of prey species like krill, preventing overpopulation and promoting the balance of the food web.
