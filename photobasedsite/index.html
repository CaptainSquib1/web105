Marine biologists also study the impacts of human activities on ocean ecosystems, including pollution, overfishing, and climate change. Their work is vital in developing strategies for the conservation and sustainable management of marine resources. By understanding the complex relationships within the marine world, marine biologists help us address urgent environmental challenges and protect the health of our oceans.
